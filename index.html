    Let's Form a Band!

    Let's Form a Band is a game made with HTML, CSS, Javascript and jQuery. The game places two players against one another, each player trying to gather the necessary goods to 'win the record deal' first.


    Dive Log

    The idea behind this project was to make an informative and useful application for scuba divers to find dive sites they would like to try, or to log their own experience at a dive site. It was made with HTML, CSS, Express, and MongoDB/Mongoose.


    Speculook

    Speculook was a group project,made for the WDIR Speculoos class of General Assembly to use as a way to keep in contact. We used HTML, CSS, and AngularJS. You may make your own account or sign in as 'Guest' with the password 'guest'.
