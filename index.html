    Laura Levenhagen

    Hello! My name is Laura Levenhagen. I'm a full-stack developer located in San Diego, California. I am passionate about providing people and businesses with a functional, aesthetically pleasing and modern site, and I have made special effort to learn and enact mobile resizing in my work.

    In addition to HTML, CSS, and Javascript, I am trained in jQuery and Ruby, as well as MongoDB, Postgresql, and the React, AngularJS, and NodeJS frameworks.

    In my free time, I am a musician, a scuba diver, and a lover of cats and literature - preferably at the same time. I enjoy community and teamwork, and I am determined to test my limits and grow my abilities every day.
